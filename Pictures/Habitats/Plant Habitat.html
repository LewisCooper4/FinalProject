        Plant Habitat

        Edit
        • History
        Comments15
        462pages on
        this wiki
        CurrentCurrent - PreviousPrevious

        “Equipped with a few flowers, some nice grass, mossy rocks and even a Happy Tree, this has everything to make a couple of Plant Dragons live in comfort. Watch them play among the ferns or soar above the trees in all their green glamour. This little patch of verdant heaven makes a great starter home for any collection of dragons.”
          — Game Description 


        Cost 100
        Building Time Instant
        Dragon Capacity 2
        Gold Capacity 200
        Sells For 50
        Experience 100
        Available Level 1
        Size 3x3
        Upgradable To Large Plant Habitat

        Notes

        • The Plant Habitat has the lowest dragoncash capacity in the game.
        • On April 17, 2013, the Plant Habitat slightly changed in appearance.
          • On April 18, 2013, the Plant Habitat changed back to its original appearance but reverted to the newer appearance on April 22, 2013.
        • On April 29, 2013, the Plant Habitat received its own unique sound upon clicking it.
